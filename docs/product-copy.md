## FAQ

**Is this financial advice?**
No. MarketPilot AI is a strategy automation tool. It does not provide financial advice, and past performance does not guarantee future results.

**Can I lose money?**
Yes. Prediction market trading involves risk of loss. Paper trading mode lets you practice without financial risk. Live trading involves real capital and real risk.

**How does paper trading work?**
Paper trading simulates real market execution using live market data. Orders are filled at realistic prices with simulated slippage. No real money is involved.

**Can I use this in my country?**
Paper trading is available everywhere. Live trading eligibility depends on your jurisdiction. The platform will check eligibility during onboarding and route you to the appropriate mode.

**What happens if my bot hits a risk limit?**
The bot pauses automatically and you receive an alert. You can review the situation and restart when ready, or adjust your risk parameters.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Downgrade or cancel at any time from your billing settings.
